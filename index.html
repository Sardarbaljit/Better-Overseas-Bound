How It Works

What to Expect in Your First Three Days

The service focuses on the tasks that matter most when you first arrive in Munich.

Day 1

Airport and Accommodation

I meet you at Munich Airport, help you manage your luggage and accompany you to your accommodation by public transport. Along the way, I explain the available ticket options and how Munich's transport system works.

Day 2

Phone and Everyday Setup

We look at suitable SIM card options, the identification you may need and the steps involved in getting connected. I also help you understand the immediate tasks that need attention after your arrival.

Day 3

Banking and Health Insurance

I explain common application routes, the documents usually required and the next steps for banking and health insurance. You choose the providers, complete the applications and sign all agreements yourself.

At the end of the service, you will have a clearer understanding of the tasks you have completed and what still needs your attention.

Frequently Asked Questions

Questions About the Service

What does the service cost?

€30, paid once, for the three day service. This is an introductory price while B.O.B works with its first students. Costs charged by others, such as transport tickets, SIM cards, bank fees and insurance premiums, are paid by you directly. There are no subscriptions or automatic charges.

What happens after I submit the form?

Nothing is charged and nothing is booked yet. I check whether I am available for your arrival date and reply using your preferred contact method, normally within 24 hours. Before any payment, you receive written confirmation of the dates, the schedule, the price and the cancellation terms. Your exact flight and accommodation details are collected separately once availability is confirmed, and the airport meeting point is agreed before you travel.

Which language is the service available in?

The service is currently available in English.

How will I recognise you at the airport?

We agree the meeting point in advance and exchange contact details. I message you when I am at the agreed spot. If it helps, we can also arrange a short video call before you travel.

Can a parent or family member join the introductory call?

Yes. Parents and family members are welcome to join the call and ask their own questions.

What happens if my flight is delayed?

Tell me as soon as you can. Reasonable delays are usually manageable. A major schedule change may need a new meeting time, depending on availability.

Can you help with my luggage?

I help you manage your bags on the journey from the airport to your accommodation. B.O.B is not a porter service, so you need to be able to travel safely with what you bring. Let me know in advance how many bags you expect and whether any are unusually large or heavy.

Can I cancel after booking?

You receive the cancellation terms in writing before you pay, together with the confirmed dates and price. Nothing is binding before that point.

Which areas do you cover?

Arrivals in Munich. I live in the Munich area and the service is delivered in person, so I cannot yet accompany students in other cities. You are still welcome to write to me.

Does my university share my information with B.O.B?

No. Universities that mention B.O.B only pass on a link. I receive nothing about you until you contact me yourself.

How is my information used?

Only to answer your inquiry and organise the service you request. Your details are not sold and are not passed to universities or other organisations for their marketing. Form delivery runs through Formspree, and unfinished form drafts stay in your own browser until you close the tab. You can ask for deletion at any time. Full details are in the Privacy Policy.

What is not included in the service?

Regulated advice and official procedures. I do not give legal, immigration, tax, financial or insurance advice, cannot guarantee the decisions of banks, insurers or authorities, and do not complete applications or sign contracts in your name. You choose your own providers throughout. Travel is by public transport; B.O.B is not a taxi or transfer service.
