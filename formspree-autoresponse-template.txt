FORMSPREE AUTORESPONSE TEMPLATE — B.O.B ARRIVAL SUPPORT
========================================================

WHAT THIS IS
This is the automatic confirmation email that students should receive after
submitting the inquiry form. It cannot be sent by the website itself: it must
be configured inside the Formspree dashboard.

SETUP INSTRUCTIONS
1. Log in at formspree.io and open your form (ID: mvzezpwo).
2. Go to the form's Workflow (or Autoresponse / Rules) settings.
3. Enable the autoresponse feature. Formspree recognises the student's address
   automatically because the form field is named exactly "email".
4. Paste the subject and body below.
5. Send ONE real test submission (write "TEST" in the comments), confirm the
   automatic reply arrives, check the spam folder too, then delete the test
   from the Formspree dashboard.

IMPORTANT
- Do not claim anywhere that the autoresponse is active until it has been
  configured and tested.
- Availability of the autoresponse feature can depend on the Formspree plan.
  If it is not available on the current plan, reply manually within 24 hours
  instead, as promised on the website.
