How It Works

After

After the three days

FAQ

Questions, answered

What does the service cost?

The introductory pilot price is €30, one-time, for all three days of personal support. It is a launch-stage price while B.O.B validates and improves the service with its first group of students. Third-party costs (transport tickets, SIM and mobile-plan costs, bank charges, insurance premiums, food, taxis and other external charges) are not included and are paid by the student. There are no subscriptions or automatic charges.

What happens after I submit the form?

Submitting the form does not create a payment obligation and does not guarantee availability. After receiving your details, I will confirm whether I am available for your arrival date and explain the next steps. Before payment, you will receive confirmation of the price, the three-day plan, the agreed start date and what the service includes.

What happens if my flight is delayed?

Please update B.O.B as soon as possible. Reasonable flight delays will be accommodated where possible, but significant schedule changes may require a revised meeting time depending on availability.

Can you carry my luggage?

I can help you manage your luggage during the journey, but students must remain able to travel safely with their belongings. Please tell me in advance how many bags you are bringing and whether any are unusually heavy.

Can I cancel after booking?

Cancellation and withdrawal information will be provided before payment and before the service begins. If you ask for the service to begin immediately, any required confirmation will be collected first.

Which cities do you cover?

The in-person service currently covers arrivals in Munich, where the founder lives. If you are arriving in another German city, B.O.B cannot yet accompany you in person, but you are welcome to get in touch.

How did my university know about B.O.B?

B.O.B is designed to be shared by universities with incoming international students, as a recommendation and nothing more. Your university never shares your data with B.O.B. Every conversation starts because you chose to reach out.

What happens to my data?

Your information, including your flight and arrival details, is used only to respond to your inquiry and coordinate the requested arrival service, and is kept only for as long as necessary for that. Your data is never sold or shared with universities or other organisations for their own marketing purposes. Selected service providers, including Formspree and the website-hosting provider, process limited personal data on behalf of B.O.B where necessary to operate the website, deliver form submissions and respond to your inquiry. Unfinished form entries are stored temporarily in your own browser (sessionStorage) and disappear when you close the tab. You can request deletion at any time by email. Full details are in the Privacy Policy.

What does B.O.B not do?

B.O.B provides personal guidance and practical assistance, not professional services. It does not make legal, financial, immigration or insurance decisions for students, sign contracts on their behalf, guarantee approval by banks, insurers or government authorities, pay third-party charges, process visas or university admissions, or complete official procedures in the student's name. It is also not a taxi or private-transport service: travel is by public transport unless another arrangement has been explicitly agreed.
