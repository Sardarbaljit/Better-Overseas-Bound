B.O.B — DESIGN AND COPY SUMMARY
================================

NEW INTERFACE DIRECTION
The site was rebuilt around clarity and restraint. Key changes:
- A thin fixed navigation bar with a translucent, blurred background and one
  clear call to action (Plan Your Arrival). The mobile menu is a simple full
  width panel that closes on Escape, on outside click, and on link tap.
- A focused hero with generous vertical space, a large headline on a controlled
  line length, a shorter and narrower paragraph, and only two buttons. No label
  sits above the hero.
- Trust information is a single restrained row of four items with light check
  marks, not heavy cards. It stacks cleanly to two columns, then one, on smaller
  screens.
- The three day service is presented as three large feature panels with big
  gradient numbers 01, 02 and 03, each with a short heading and concise text,
  in a clean vertical sequence that is easy to scan.
- Pricing is a single panel with a large, easy to find €30, a short set of
  included items, a compact external costs block, and a one line limitation
  that links to the FAQs rather than dominating the panel.
- The founder section is one photograph and three short paragraphs.
- The FAQ is a refined accordion with generous spacing, large touch targets and
  a rotating chevron.
- The form keeps its two step structure with a progress bar, comfortable field
  sizing and concise helper text.
- Motion is limited to a subtle IntersectionObserver reveal, and it is disabled
  under prefers-reduced-motion. Colours (blue and purple) are used selectively,
  mostly on the logo, numbers, primary buttons and small accents.
- System font stack only. No external fonts, frameworks, analytics or trackers.
