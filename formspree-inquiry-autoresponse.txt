FORMSPREE AUTORESPONSE — PUBLIC INQUIRY FORM
=============================================

SETUP
1. Log in at formspree.io and open the INQUIRY form (ID: mvzezpwo — the form
   used by index.html).
2. Open that form's Workflow / Autoresponse / Rules settings.
3. Enable the autoresponse. Formspree finds the student's address automatically
   because the form field is named exactly "email".
4. Paste the subject and body below.
5. Send ONE real test submission (write "TEST" in the comments), confirm the
   reply arrives (check spam too), then delete the test in the dashboard.

Do not claim the autoresponse is active until it has been configured and
tested. If the current Formspree plan does not include autoresponses, reply
manually within 24 hours instead, as promised on the website.
