B.O.B — IMAGE GUIDE
====================
The website shows photos in the hero and the three day cards. Save each image
with the exact filename below and upload it to the SAME folder as index.html
(the repo root, next to founder-photo.jpg). Filenames are case sensitive.

Until a file is added, that spot shows a soft neutral grey placeholder, so the
site never looks broken.

IMAGE NOTES AND FUTURE REPLACEMENTS
- munich.jpg is the strongest image and is well suited to the hero. Keep it.
  Crop towards Marienplatz and the historic centre, and reduce the amount of
  empty sky. The layout already positions the crop lower using object-position.
- transport.jpg and sim.jpg are TEMPORARY pilot images. They are darker and
  more dramatic than the hero, so they sit slightly apart in style. Replace
  them, when you can, with brighter, more natural photographs (for example, a
  bright daytime tram or U-Bahn, and a clean, well lit SIM or phone setup).
- banking.jpg is acceptable for the pilot but is a little busy. It may later be
  replaced with a cleaner banking setup image (for example, a card and a laptop
  or a simple desk scene). Crop towards the cards and only a few coins.
- All three day images are shown at the same 16:10 ratio, the same corner
  radius and the same crop behaviour, so they already look consistent as a set.

RECOMMENDED DIMENSIONS AND COMPRESSION
- munich.jpg: about 1960 px wide (shown at 16:8).
- transport.jpg, sim.jpg, banking.jpg: about 1000 px wide (shown at 16:10).
- Save as JPEG at roughly 80% quality. Aim for under about 300 KB each; the
  hero can be a little larger. Free tool: squoosh.app (drag in, export).
- Choose a crop where the main subject sits near the centre so it works on
  mobile as well as desktop.

IMAGE RIGHTS (IMPORTANT)
Only publish images that B.O.B has the right to use commercially. If any photo
came from a stock library, check that its licence permits use on a live,
business website. The HTML repeats this reminder in a comment beside every
image reference.
