FORMSPREE AUTORESPONSE — CONFIRMED-BOOKING FORM
================================================

SETUP
1. First create the SEPARATE Formspree form for confirmed booking details
   (it does not exist yet). Copy its endpoint URL.
2. Replace REPLACE_WITH_BOOKING_FORM_ENDPOINT in booking-details.html with
   that URL. Until then, the booking page deliberately refuses to submit.
3. In that new form's Workflow / Autoresponse / Rules settings, enable the
   autoresponse (the student field is named exactly "email") and paste the
   subject and body below.
4. Send ONE real test submission, confirm the reply arrives (check spam too),
   then delete the test in the dashboard.

Do not claim the autoresponse is active until it has been configured and
tested.
