2. What data we collect

When you submit the contact form, we receive the information you enter: your name, email address, WhatsApp number, nationality, university, degree program, intake month, expected arrival date, city in Germany, the topics you need help with, and any message you write. You provide this information voluntarily.

Our hosting provider (Vercel Inc.) automatically processes technical data such as your IP address in server logs to deliver the website securely.

3. Why we collect it

Your form data is used for exactly one purpose: to contact you and provide the relocation guidance service you requested. The legal basis is your consent and the preparation of a contract with you (Art. 6(1)(a) and (b) GDPR). We do not use your data for advertising, we do not sell it, and we do not share it with universities or any other third party for their purposes.

4. Form delivery via Formspree

The contact form is delivered by Formspree, Inc. (USA), which transmits your submission to us by email and may briefly store it to provide that service. Formspree processes this data on our behalf. You can read Formspree's own privacy policy at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

5. Fonts

This website loads the Inter typeface from Google Fonts. When the page loads, your browser connects to Google servers, which involves the transfer of your IP address to Google. Details are available in Google's privacy policy.

6. How long we keep your data

Form submissions are kept only as long as needed to provide the service and are deleted no later than 12 months after your last contact with us, unless legal retention obligations require otherwise.

7. Your rights

  • You can request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • You can request correction of inaccurate data.
  • You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing us. We will confirm the deletion.
  • You can withdraw your consent at any time with effect for the future.
  • You have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority, for example the Bavarian State Office for Data Protection Supervision (BayLDA).

8. No data from universities

Universities do not send us any student data. Every conversation with B.O.B begins because you chose to contact us yourself.
