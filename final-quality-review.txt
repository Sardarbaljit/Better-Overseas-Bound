FINAL FOUR SERVICE CARDS (no day labels anywhere)
Airport to Accommodation — I meet you at Munich Airport, help you manage your
luggage and travel with you to your accommodation by public transport. During
the journey, I explain the ticket options and how to use Munich's transport
network.

Phone and Everyday Setup — We review SIM card options, the identification you
may need and the steps involved in getting connected. I also help you understand
which immediate tasks need attention after you arrive.

Banking — I explain the usual routes and documents for opening a student bank
account. You choose the provider, complete the application and sign any
agreement yourself.

Health Insurance — I explain how student health insurance usually works, which
documents may be required and how to begin the application process. You choose
the insurer, complete the application and sign any agreement yourself.

FINAL THREE DAY TIMING NOTE (below the cards)
The service is arranged around your arrival and completed across the agreed
three day period.
