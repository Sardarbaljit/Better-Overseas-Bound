B.O.B — FINAL REVIEW SUMMARY
=============================

FINAL COLOUR SYSTEM

  --ink #1D1D1F, --ink-soft #6E6E73, --ink-faint #86868B
  --background #FFFFFF, --background-soft #F5F5F7, --border #D2D2D7

Primary buttons and the navigation button are solid blue (#2563EB, hover

the accent details and links use solid blue. Section backgrounds alternate
white and #F5F5F7, with the pricing panel on a dark tile.

the navigation, hero text, day markers, the progress area (now gone), the
pricing decoration and all large backgrounds.

FINAL DAY COPY
Day 1 — Airport and Accommodation
I meet you at Munich Airport, help you manage your luggage and travel with you
to your accommodation by public transport. During the journey, I explain the
available ticket options and how to use Munich's transport network.

Day 2 — Phone and Everyday Setup
We review SIM card options, the identification you may need and the steps
involved in getting connected. I also help you understand which immediate tasks
need attention after you arrive.

Day 3 — Banking and Health Insurance
I explain the usual application routes and documents for opening a bank account
and arranging health insurance. You choose the providers, complete the
applications and sign all agreements yourself.

Outcome line below the panels:
At the end of the service, you will have a clearer understanding of what has
been completed and what still needs your attention.

Email-only submissions work without a WhatsApp number; choosing WhatsApp or
Email and WhatsApp requires a valid number, with an inline message and focus on
the field. JavaScript references only the fields that still exist.

IMAGE TREATMENT
Hero: munich.jpg in a wide rounded container (22px radius), subtle shadow, no
overlay, cropped lower via object-position to favour the square and reduce sky.
Loaded eagerly with fetchpriority high and width/height to limit layout shift.
Day cards: transport.jpg, sim.jpg and banking.jpg all share one presentation —
same 16:10 ratio, same corner radius, same cover crop, lazy loaded, with
width/height set. Every image has a neutral grey gradient fallback if missing
and an HTML comment reminding that only commercially licensed images may be
published.
