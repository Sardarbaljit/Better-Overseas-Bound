B.O.B — IMAGE GUIDE
====================
The website now shows photos in the hero and the three day cards. Save each
image with the exact filename below and upload it to the SAME folder as
index.html (the repo root, next to founder-photo.jpg). Filenames are
case sensitive and must match exactly.

Until a file is added, that spot shows a soft blue/purple gradient placeholder,
so the site never looks broken.

PREPARING THE FILES (recommended, not required)
- Resize munich.jpg to about 1800 px wide; the three card images to about
  1000 px wide. This keeps the page fast.
- Save as JPEG at roughly 80% quality. Aim for under ~350 KB each.
- Free tools: squoosh.app (drag in, export) or any photo app's "export for web".
- The hero image is shown at 16:8 and the card images at 16:10, cropped to fill.
  Choose a crop where the main subject sits near the centre.

RIGHTS
Only upload photos you are allowed to use. If any came from a stock library,
check its licence permits use on a live website.
