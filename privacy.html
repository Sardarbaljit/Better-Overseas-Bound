2. What information we process

When you submit the inquiry form, we receive the information you enter: your name, email address, WhatsApp number, university, city of study, the country you are travelling from, study intake, flight arrival date, scheduled landing time, arrival terminal, airline and flight number (optional), number of suitcases, approximate accommodation area or postcode, your preferred contact method, your preferred language, the services or concerns you select, and any message you write. You provide this information voluntarily.

Flight and arrival details (date, landing time, terminal, flight number, number of suitcases and approximate accommodation area) are used only to coordinate the requested arrival service, for example planning the airport meeting and the journey to your accommodation. We deliberately do not ask for your complete street address in the public form; exact meeting and accommodation details are confirmed privately later.

If you open the website through a link shared by a university or a specific campaign, the form also records where the link came from (a referral source and, where applicable, a campaign name). This tells us only which link you used. It contains no personal information about you, and it does not mean the university sent us any of your data.

Our hosting provider automatically processes technical data such as your IP address in server logs in order to deliver the website securely.

3. Why we process it, and who else is involved

Your information is used for one purpose: to respond to your inquiry and provide the personal arrival and settling-in service you requested. The legal basis is your consent and the preparation of a contract with you (Art. 6(1)(a) and (b) GDPR).

Your data is never sold or shared with universities or other organisations for their own marketing purposes. Selected service providers, including Formspree and the website-hosting provider, process limited personal data on behalf of B.O.B where necessary to operate the website, deliver form submissions and respond to your inquiry.

4. Form delivery via Formspree

The inquiry form is delivered by Formspree, Inc. (USA), which transmits your submission to us by email and may briefly store it to provide that service. You can read Formspree's own privacy policy at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

5. Form drafts in your browser

While you fill in the inquiry form, your unfinished entries are stored temporarily in your own browser (sessionStorage) so they are not lost if the page reloads. This draft never leaves your device, is not sent to us, and is deleted automatically when you close the browser tab. Your Privacy Policy acknowledgement is never stored. The website does not use cookies, analytics, tracking pixels or external fonts.

6. Communication through WhatsApp

Using WhatsApp is optional. In the form you can choose whether you prefer to be contacted by WhatsApp, by email, or either. If you choose email, WhatsApp will not be used.

If you choose WhatsApp, your phone number and the messages we exchange are processed through WhatsApp, which operates under its own privacy terms (see WhatsApp's privacy policy). B.O.B uses WhatsApp only to communicate with you about the requested service.

7. Communication through email

If you choose email, or if you contact us by email directly, your email address and the content of our correspondence are processed by the email providers involved in delivering the messages. Email is used only to communicate with you about your inquiry and the requested service.

8. Data processed outside Germany or the EEA

Some of the service providers named above may process data outside Germany or the European Economic Area. For example, Formspree is based in the USA, and WhatsApp is operated by a company group with locations outside the EEA. Where that happens, the providers state that they apply safeguards under their own privacy terms. If you prefer to avoid this, you can contact us by email instead of using the form or WhatsApp.

9. How long we keep your data

Your information is kept only for as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and provide the requested service, unless legal retention obligations require otherwise. After that it is deleted.

10. Your rights

  • You can request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • You can request correction of inaccurate data.
  • You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing us at the address in section 1. We will confirm the deletion.
  • You can withdraw your consent at any time with effect for the future.
  • You have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority, for example the Bavarian State Office for Data Protection Supervision (BayLDA).

11. No data from universities

Universities do not send us any student data. Every conversation with B.O.B begins because you chose to contact us yourself.
