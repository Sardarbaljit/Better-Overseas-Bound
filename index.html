FAQ

Questions, answered

Is €30 really the full price?

Yes. €30 is a one-time, flat fee covering one one-on-one consultation, a personalized arrival checklist, and 3 days of hands-on integration assistance on WhatsApp. If you need more support after that, just ask about extending. There are never subscriptions, upsells, or automatic charges.

Which cities do you cover?

B.O.B currently focuses on students arriving in Munich, where the founder lives and knows the system firsthand. Coming to another German city? Reach out anyway: much of the guidance (insurance, banking, bureaucracy) applies across Germany, and coverage is expanding.

When should I get in touch?

Ideally before you fly, since the biggest time savings come from arriving prepared. But if you've already landed and feel lost, it's absolutely not too late. Reach out and we'll sort out your situation from wherever you are.

How does the guidance actually happen?

After you fill in the form, you'll get a reply within 24 hours to schedule your one-on-one consultation. From there you receive a personal plan for your arrival, and during your 3-day integration period you can message on WhatsApp whenever questions come up.

How did my university know about B.O.B?

B.O.B is designed to be shared by universities with incoming international students, as a recommendation and nothing more. Your university never shares your data with B.O.B. Every conversation starts because you chose to reach out.

What happens to my data?

Your information is used only to provide the service, nothing else. Form submissions are delivered by Formspree and stored no longer than 12 months. Your data is never sold or shared with third parties, and you can request deletion at any time by email. Full details are in the Privacy Policy.

Is B.O.B a visa or admissions agency?

No. B.O.B doesn't process visas or university applications. It focuses on what happens around your move: settling in, bureaucracy, banking, insurance, transport, and everyday life. That's the part no one else guides you through.
